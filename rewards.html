How to Provide LP on QuickSwap

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet
Visit QuickSwap and click “Connect Wallet” in the top-right corner. Choose your preferred wallet, such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Make sure your wallet is connected to the Polygon Network.

Step 2: Add $HROOM / SPR and WETH
In QuickSwap, navigate to the Pool section and select V2. Search for the $HROOM/WETH or SPR/WETH pairs. If they don’t appear, paste the contract addresses provided below. Enter the amount you'd like to supply, approve the tokens, click “Supply,” and confirm the transaction in your wallet. You’ll receive LP tokens as confirmation.

Contract Addresses:

Shroom ($HROOM):
0x924B16Dfb993EEdEcc91c6D08b831e94135dEaE1

Spore (SPR):
0x089582AC20ea563c69408a79E1061de594b61bED

Ethereum (WETH):
0x7ceB23fD6bC0adD59E62ac25578270cFf1b9f61

Step 3: Managing Your LP Tokens
Once you’ve added liquidity, you will receive LP tokens, which represent your share in the pool. These LP tokens are used to track your contribution to the liquidity pool. Here's what you can do with your LP tokens:

  • Adding more liquidity: You can always add more liquidity to the pool at any time by following the same steps as before.
  • Removing liquidity: If you wish to withdraw your liquidity, go to the "Remove Liquidity" section on QuickSwap, select your LP tokens, and confirm the withdrawal.
  • Staking LP Tokens: You can stake your LP tokens on platforms that support staking to earn additional rewards, such as farming rewards or token incentives.
