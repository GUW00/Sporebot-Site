🛠️ Sporebot & Reddit Integration

🌱 Daily Forage Threads

A new forage thread is posted automatically every day at 4:20 EST. You can earn rewards by commenting `!forage` on the current day's thread. Foraging helps increase your streak, leading to higher rewards.

💧 Faucet System

New users can claim a one-time bonus of 1,000 SHROOMS and 100,000 SPORES by using the `!faucet` command, provided they meet the karma/account age requirements. This bonus is only available once.

🧺 Forage Rewards

Foraging rewards are based on your streak:

  • Day 1 starts at 1,000 SHROOMS.
  • Up to 6,874 SHROOMS and 687,400 SPORES can be earned daily.

🔥 Forage Streak Progression

As your streak grows, so do your rewards. Here’s how the rewards break down:

  • 1–6 days: 1,000 → 1,090 SHROOMS | 100,000 → 109,000 SPORES
  • 7–29 days: 1,105 → 1,435 SHROOMS | 110,500 → 143,500 SPORES | Bronze Shroomie Flair
  • 30–59 days: 1,450 → 1,865 SHROOMS | 145,000 → 186,500 SPORES | Silver Shroomie Flair
  • 60–99 days: 1,880 → 2,305 SHROOMS | 188,000 → 230,500 SPORES | Gold Shroomie Flair
  • 100–168 days: 2,320 → 6,874 SHROOMS | 232,000 → 687,400 SPORES | Platinum Shroomie Flair
  • 169+ days: 6,874 SHROOMS | 687,400 SPORES | Diamond Shroomie Flair

🔗 Wallet Linking & Cross-Rewards

Link your Reddit account to Discord and verify your Polygon wallet to earn 50,000 SHROOMS and 5,000,000 SPORES. You can also transfer your Reddit Off-Chain SHROOMS to Discord and withdraw them on-chain.

🌾 Farm System

Your Farm Rank impacts your SHROOM planting limit and APY bonus. Here are the available ranks and their bonuses:

  • BRONZE: 0% bonus, 2,000,000 SHROOMS limit
  • SILVER: +3% APY, 2,500,000 SHROOMS limit
  • GOLD: +6% APY, 3,000,000 SHROOMS limit
  • EMERALD: +9% APY, 3,500,000 SHROOMS limit
  • DIAMOND: +12% APY, 4,200,000 SHROOMS limit

🌿 Farm Growth Stages

Your farm evolves through 6 stages, each increasing your SPORE generation:

  • Inactive: 0% SPORE bonus
  • Spore Stage: 4% bonus (Day 1)
  • Germination Stage: 8% bonus (Day 5)
  • Mycelium Stage: 12% bonus (Day 15)
  • Fruiting Stage: 16% bonus (Day 25)
  • Sporulation Stage: 20% bonus (Day 42)

🏅 Farm Upgrades

Once you reach the Sporulation Stage, you can harvest your farm. Harvesting allows you to:

  • Increase your farm rank
  • Earn a permanent 3% yield bonus
  • Increase your planting limit

🌿 Farm Regression

To prevent regression, ensure you mist or fan your farm at least once every 3 days. Failing to do so will send your farm back to an earlier stage.

🌲 Forage Rewards

Foraging builds a streak, unlocking special items and bonuses:

  • Day 7: Unlock the Golden Shovel for the `!dig` command.
  • Day 21: Unlock the Queen Totem for bonus SHROOM rewards.
  • Day 42: Unlock the King Totem for bonus SHROOM rewards.
  • Day 69: Unlock the Kid Totem for bonus SPORE rewards.
  • Day 100: Unlock the Gold Totem for a chance at an extra `!toss`.

🧙 Visit System Upgrades

The Wimblecap system provides more opportunities to interact:

  • 14 visits: Unlock the `!flute`, which restores your forage streak if missed within 3 days.
  • 50 visits: Unlock the `!pipe` for even more rewards.
  • Wimblecap also explains all collected artifacts and scrolls.

⛏️ Dig Command System

The digging system allows you to:

  • Register your wallet and start treasure hunting.
  • Treasure rewards come from a live pool, which includes BTC, ETH, NFTs, and more.
  • Unlock 3 chest tiers:
    • Bronze: 50,000 SHROOMS
    • Silver: 100,000 SHROOMS
    • Golden: 1,000,000 SHROOMS + a bonus pool (One winner per month)

🧪 Potions & Brewing

The Brewing System unlocks over time based on your farm rank:

  • Bronze: Unlock basic ingredients like Sunspore and Glowcap.
  • Silver: Brew with 3 ingredients.
  • Gold: Unlock ingredients like Frostmoss and Veilfruit.
  • Emerald: Brew with 4 ingredients, and discover secret recipes.
  • Diamond: Unlock the top-tier ingredients, like Bloodberry.

⚠️ Potion Rotation

Once brewed, your potion will affect your farm performance. Potions can only be changed every 3 days.

🎁 Airdrop System

To participate in airdrop events, you must now be verified. Verification ensures that only legitimate users can claim rewards, avoiding bot abuse.

The raffle system has been streamlined. To participate:

📈 XP & Activity Tracking

Earn XP for actions such as:

  • Chatting: 1 XP per 5 characters.

  • Daily Streaks: You can earn up to 6,874 SHROOMS per day for high XP streaks.

💼 Goodshroom Tokens

You can earn Goodshroom Tokens (GS) every time you level up. GS can be gifted to others for additional rewards.

📜 Transparency & Logs

Everything in Sporebot is trackable and transparent. This includes actions like staking, farming, rewards, and NFT earnings. Full audit logs are available for users to track progress and rewards.

🏆 Live Leaderboards

Track your progress with live leaderboards that display:

  • Social Butterfly

💸 Withdrawals

You can now withdraw SHROOMS and SPORES to your wallet instantly. This is done via the `!withdraw` command, but requires Pixie verification and linking your wallet with `!register`.

💸 Ultimate Guide to Providing LP on QuickSwap

🔗 Connecting Your Wallet

To provide liquidity, you first need to connect your wallet to QuickSwap:

  • Visit QuickSwap
  • Click on “Connect Wallet” in the top-right corner.
  • Select your preferred wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.).
  • Ensure your wallet is connected to the Polygon Network.

💰 Adding $HROOM, SPR, and WETH to LP

Now that your wallet is connected, you can add liquidity to the QuickSwap LP:

  • Navigate to the Pool section of QuickSwap.
  • Select the V2 pool and search for the $HROOM/WETH or SPR/WETH pairs.
  • If they don't appear, paste the contract addresses provided below.
  • Enter the amount you'd like to supply, approve the tokens, and click “Supply”.
  • Confirm the transaction in your wallet to provide liquidity.
  • You will receive LP tokens as confirmation of your liquidity provision.

📜 Contract Addresses

Here are the contract addresses for the tokens you’ll be adding to the liquidity pool:

  • Shroom ($HROOM): VIEW CONTRACT
  • Spore (SPR): VIEW CONTRACT
  • Ethereum (WETH): VIEW CONTRACT

🔄 Managing Your LP Tokens

Once you've provided liquidity, you’ll receive LP tokens. These represent your share in the pool. Here’s how to manage them:

  • Adding more liquidity: You can always add more liquidity to the pool by following the same steps mentioned above.
  • Removing liquidity: If you wish to withdraw your liquidity, simply go to the "Remove Liquidity" section on QuickSwap, choose your tokens, and confirm the withdrawal.

🤖 Sporebot vs. CommunityCurrency (CCBot)

Sporebot handles !forage, !faucet, and !link commands inside the r/MushroomPlanet Daily Thread.
CCBot (CommunityCurrency bot) handles wallet management and tips across Reddit broadly.

  • Sporebot = MushroomPlanet only (connects with Discord + rewards).
  • 🤖 CCBot = Reddit-wide tipping & on-chain withdrawals.

Here’s how to use CCBot:

  • !register 0xYourWallet — Register your Polygon address
  • !tip 100 or !tip 100 SHROOM — Tip users
  • !withdraw 100 SHROOM — Withdraw to your wallet
  • !faucet — Claim a one-time reward
